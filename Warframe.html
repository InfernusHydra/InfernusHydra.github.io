Planets

This game takes place on multiple planets depending on which you go to and what mission your doing on the planets, Earth, Mercury, Venus, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Sedna, Phobos, Uranus, europa, Eris, Neptune, Ceres, and Pluto with bosses to defeat on each planet to get to the next planet. Also the Void and Orokin Derelict which aren't really planets.

Bosses

Bosses are defeated for Xp and frame parts or to gain access to the next planet for reasources.

Earth

A long destoryed land ruled by the Grineer. The boss on Earth is Councilor Vay Hek a geneticly modified Grineer

Mercury

A home base for the Grineer. With the first boss you shall fight Captain Vor who's also a modified Grineer, thought not as modified as Councilor Vay Hek.

Venus

A extractor planet for the Corpus. Their boss is the Jackal a deadly creation of the Corpus that has killed many warframes.

Mars

A camp planet for the Corpus. Seargent Nef Anyo is the boss for Mars he is only a regular Corpus Crewman only with a bit better damage and shield.

Jupiter

The main base for the Corpus. Many Warframes have been disassembled to make the Zanuka a deadly Robot invented by the mad genius Alad V.

Saturn

Another extraction planet for the Grineer. The boss of Saturn is another modified Grineer called General Sargas Ruk with a specialty for fire attacks.
